Description:
This project serves as a building block in a creative/research project underway at conestoga college. The project serves to further development of a web-based mobile application enabling users to benefit from modern accessibility developments of VR technology, making it accessible for most modern mobile devices with the use of a VR headset attachment (Current major examples include Google Cardboard headsets). Further development is intended for use in mental health applications to provide a safe methods of self-administering exposure-therapy to familliarize the user with an anxiety provoking environment to prepare for real world encounters and ease the transition.
